Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Google account ID (hashed for privacy)
  • Display name
  • Game statistics and progress
  • User-generated content (levels, objects, functions)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide game services and multiplayer functionality
  • Store your game progress and statistics
  • Enable sharing of user-generated content
  • Maintain account security through session management

3. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Hashing of Google account IDs
  • Secure session management with HMAC-style authentication
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS)
  • Server-side secret for additional security

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers. Google account IDs are hashed and cannot be reversed to reveal your actual Google ID.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Your Google account information is handled according to Google's privacy policy.
